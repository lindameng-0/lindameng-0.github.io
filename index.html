Black Swan

A Memokeeper of the Garden of Recollection. A mysterious and elegant soothsayer.

She often wears a warm smile and is willing to patiently listen to the words of others, thus using such means as a pretext to enter "memories" and gain omniscience over certain matters. A lady passionate about collecting unique memories, yet the thoughts that guide her are hard to glean.
